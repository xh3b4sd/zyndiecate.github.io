why that blog?

TL;DR

  • documentation of https://chatstorm.de
  • sharing knowlegde

long version

I have started a fun project, to use all that stuff I have learned for something. There were not that much ideas and one of that less was a simple chat. When I talk about a chat I mean a digital location where peoples are online and you are able to talk to anybody you want, in real-time. That is why facebook is not a chat. Facebook is a network providing a messanger, were you can interact with people you already know. A chat enables you to interact with people you not already know. Over the time I recognized that there are thousand of chats, and none of them is doing good in that what it is doing. So my motivation for my own chat was to look behind the scenes and to try to make it better than others. Further it matches what I am looking for and what I am able to provide. So, my project is ChatStorm and there I was, and still am, doing a lot of stuff I want to document and want to share with others interessted in. My focus is not necessarily to earn money or get rich with it, but maybe that will be a nice side effect at some point. Whatever, all that is the reason to create that blog. So enjoy and maybe support, contribute or communicate in some way. Go for it.
